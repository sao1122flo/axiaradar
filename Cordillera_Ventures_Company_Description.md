## 3. Long description (~180 words) — for one-pagers, partner intros, Stripe verification

> Cordillera Ventures LLC is a privately held holding company organized under the laws of the State of Delaware and wholly owned by its founder and sole member, Sergio Ordonez.
>
> The company serves as the parent entity for a focused portfolio of independently operated digital businesses. Its current portfolio includes:
>
> - **Flo** ([miflo.co](https://www.miflo.co)) — a financial-data platform that aggregates digital-invoice activity across Latin American markets and packages it into anonymized market-trend intelligence for businesses, helping companies see demand, pricing, and category dynamics they couldn't otherwise observe.
> - **Axia Radar** ([axiaradar.com](https://axiaradar.com)) — a market-intelligence tool built for freelancers and independent professionals, giving solo operators the kind of demand, rate, and positioning data usually reserved for larger firms.
>
> Cordillera Ventures' strategy is to build and operate niche, self-funded software businesses that can grow profitably without outside capital. The holding company centralizes ownership, banking, accounting, payments, and contracting infrastructure across the portfolio, while each operating brand retains full ownership of its product roadmap, customer relationships, and market positioning.
>
> The company is privately held, fully bootstrapped, and not currently raising outside investment.
